1. Hillsong Worship.

This band tops my list because of it's diverse talent and their everyday innovations in their music. Hillsong Worship band's music has really come in handy in my christian walk. Their songs have been a pillar to my devotions and have also evoked essential christian lessons.

Who are they? Here is their biography.

Hillsong is a popular Christian rock and worship group operating out of Australia's Hillsong Church, a Pentecostal ministry based in northwest Sydney. Founded in August 1983 by New Zealand natives Brian and Bobbie Houston, Hillsong Church was first known as Hills Christian Life Centre.

Who are they? Here is their biography.

Casting Crowns is a contemporary Christian and Christian rock band started in 1999 by youth pastor Mark Hall, who serves as the band's lead vocalist, as part of a youth group at First Baptist Church in Downtown Daytona Beach, Florida. Christian rock group Casting Crowns began as a student worship band in Daytona Beach, Florida, in 1999. Led by singer, songwriter, and youth pastor Mark Hall, the group initially included guitarists Juan DeVevo and Hector Cervantes, violinist Melodee DeVevo, and drummer Rob Cervantes (a.k.a. Chavez).

3. H_art The Band.

This Uliza Kiatu band potrays an array of brilliance on the way they combine their different voices to produce a comforting music. Also, H_art The Band dress to their fan's fashiong making them youth's favourite. At times, you would confuse their music to 'Spoken Word'.

Well, who are they? Here is their biography.

H_art The Band is made up of Wachira Gatama, Kenneth Muya and Mordecai Mwini as published by The Standard on the year 2020. It was formed in 2012 when the trio met at KNT during a play on cancer awareness. With only few years in the Kenyan music industry, they have already been listed among the top 5 artists in terms of radio airplay by the Music Copyright Society of Kenya.
